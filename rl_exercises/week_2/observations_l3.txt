Our Contextual marsROver has four different context features:
    "slip_termination_probability": 0.2,
    "slip_penalty": -20.0,
    "left_reward": 3.0,
    "right_reward": 10.0,

But only slip_termination_probability and right_reward are currently changed over different runs.
The idea is that the right state is terminal and has the higher reward but taking hte action right is more risky then going for the safe(r) choice of taking the lower reward in the leftmost terminal state.
If the agent "slips" the episode terminates with a negative reward.
We use validation to test interpolation and test to test extrapolation and tried 3 different train sets (modes a to c from section 6.4).
